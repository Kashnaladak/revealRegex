Repetition & Quantifiers

X*

"X, zero or more times"

X+

"X, one or more times"

X?

"X, zero or one time"

X{n,m}

"X, at least 'n' times and at most 'm' times"

X{n,}

"X, at least 'n' times"

X{n}

"X, exactly 'n' times"

There are 3 flavors of quantifiers

Greedy (easy to understand)

Reluctant (also easy)

Possessive (omg... the horror)

Greedy quantifiers try to match as much as possible

Reluctant quantifiers try to match as little as possible

Possessive quantifiers try to match as much as possible, but only look at each character once
